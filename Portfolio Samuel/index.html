Services Offered

Podcast Audio Editing

Expert podcast audio editing using Adobe Audition, Audacity, FL Studio and more. I enhance audio quality, remove noise, add music, and deliver professional, engaging episodes ready for publishing.

Podcast Hosting & Distribution

I set up podcast hosting (Anchor, Buzzsprout, Libsyn, Podbean, Acast) and ensure smooth distribution to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Amazon Music and more, helping your show reach the widest audience possible.

Video Editing

I provide creative and technical video editing: trimming, color grading, transitions, and syncing with audio. From YouTube content to promotional reels, I ensure your videos are polished and compelling.

Website Design & Development

I design and build responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and Python. Whether it’s a portfolio, business site, or custom web app, I create modern, user-friendly solutions tailored to your needs.

Frontend & Backend Engineering

I build full-stack web applications, handling both the user interface and the server logic. On the frontend, I craft responsive, interactive experiences using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. On the backend, I develop robust systems with Node.js, Python, and databases, ensuring your site is fast, secure, and scalable.

Cloud & DevOps

I design and manage cloud infrastructure with a focus on scalability, security, and automation. Using tools like AWS, Docker, and CI/CD pipelines, I ensure smooth deployments and reliable performance for modern applications.
